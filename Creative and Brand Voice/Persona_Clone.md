The Persona Clone

Overview

A style replication system designed to analyze writing patterns and recreate them while discussing a different topic.

The objective is to capture voice, rhythm, sentence structure, and stylistic tendencies without copying original content.

Core Prompt

Analyze the writing style of:

[Text]

Then write a new piece about:

[Topic]

Requirements:

- Match sentence rhythm
- Match tone and pacing
- Match stylistic tendencies
- Preserve the overall voice
- Do not copy phrases directly
- Generate original content



Example Output

Input

Writing Sample:
Short, punchy, opinion-driven writing.
