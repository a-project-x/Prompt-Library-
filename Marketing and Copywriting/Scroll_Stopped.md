# 🎯 Prompt Name: The Scroll-Stopper

## 🧠 Purpose
Generate pattern-interrupt social media hooks designed to stop scrolling and increase engagement.



## ⚡ Prompt Psychology
This prompt works because it uses:
- Pattern interruption
- Curiosity gaps
- Counter-intuitive statements
- Emotional specificity



## 📝 The Prompt
Analyze the product: [Product] and audience: [Audience]. Write a 1-sentence social media hook. Avoid questions like "Want to save money?". Use a "Pattern Interrupt" or a counter-intuitive statement that challenges common beliefs.



## 📊 Before vs After

### Before (Generic AI)
"Are you tired of losing money on Facebook ads? Try our new tool today!"

### After (My Logic)
"Most marketers waste 40% of their ad budget on the exact same setting. Here is why turning it off wins."
