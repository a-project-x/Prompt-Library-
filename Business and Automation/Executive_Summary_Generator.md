Executive Summary Generator

Overview

A business reporting system designed to compress lengthy reports into concise executive-level insights focused on decisions and actions.

The objective is to reduce information overload while preserving strategic context.



Framework

Information Intake

↓

Key Findings

↓

Risk Assessment

↓

Action Prioritization

↓

Executive Summary



Prompt Architecture

Business Report
↓
Key Insight Extraction
↓
Roadblock Identification
↓
Action Prioritization
↓
Executive Summary



Core Prompt

Analyze this business report:

[Text]

Generate:

- 1 core summary sentence
- 3 major roadblocks
- 2 recommended action items

Requirements:

- No unnecessary detail
- No filler language
- Focus on decision-making
- Prioritize business impact



Example Output

Input

Quarterly report showing strong user growth but declining customer retention.

Output

Core Summary:

User acquisition is growing, but retention issues threaten long-term revenue growth.

Roadblocks:

1. High churn rate
2. Weak onboarding process
3. Limited customer engagement

Action Items:

1. Improve onboarding experience
2. Launch retention-focused initiatives
