Customer Support Empathy Engine

Overview

A customer support response system designed to acknowledge frustration, clarify accountability, and communicate next steps without sounding defensive or scripted.

The focus is on restoring trust while maintaining operational clarity.



Framework

Acknowledge

↓

Validate

↓

Clarify

↓

Resolve

↓

Reassure



Prompt Architecture

Customer Complaint
↓
Failure Identification
↓
Empathy Response
↓
Resolution Plan
↓
Timeline Communication



Core Prompt

An angry customer wrote:

[Customer Complaint]

Requirements:

- Acknowledge the specific failure first
- Avoid generic apologies
- Explain next steps clearly
- Provide a realistic resolution timeline
- Keep the tone calm and professional

Output:

1. Acknowledgment
2. Resolution Plan
3. Timeline
4. Closing Response



Example Output

Input

Customer reports delayed delivery and lack of communication.

Output

You're right to be frustrated. We failed to provide an update when your shipment was delayed, and that's on us.

Our team is reviewing the shipment status now and will provide a confirmed update within 24 hours.
